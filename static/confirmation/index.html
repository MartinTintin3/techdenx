What happens next

3

What to prepare

Have this information ready for the intake form:

  • Domain name(s) — The domain(s) you send email from
  • DNS host — Where your DNS is managed (e.g., Cloudflare, GoDaddy, Route53, Namecheap)
  • Email platforms — All services that send email on your behalf (e.g., Google Workspace, Mailchimp, Postmark, HubSpot, your app's transactional sender)
  • DNS access method — Your preference:
    • Best: Delegated/limited DNS access (you add us temporarily)
    • Good: Screen-share where you make changes with our guidance
    • Avoid: Sharing passwords (we don't recommend this)
